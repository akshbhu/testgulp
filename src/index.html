About Us

Dezzio is a home for collaboration between creative teams that add value to digital assets. Our vetted marketplace covers services in photography, video, visualisations, copywriting and other related services. Dezzio's key partnership with Imagecloud (www.imagecloud.tv) makes these services available to photographers, agencies and other clients.

Vision & Mission

Dezzio's plans to grow as a multi-genre, global markteplace for providing systems, processes and services to clients that require digital assets to be improved through editing, retouching, visualisations, virtual staging, copywriting etc. We plan to expand into all genres including Architectural, Events, Lifestyle, Real Estate, eCommerce, Fashion, Portraits, Travel, Commercial etc.
Through partnership integration and with out own tech, we plan to be the leader in not only introducing clients to creative teams, but also to support the workflow, systems and processes that help them work efficiently together.
By nature a creative services marketplace requires humans, but we invest in R&D to build Machine Learning models that will alleviate some of the repeatable tasks, either for clients to consume directly or for or marketplace members to use in order to make them more efficient and deliver better value. As more AI services become available and relevant to our users, we plan to integrate them where possible to simplify the entire process of managing a range of Aritificial Intelligence (AI) tools

Research & Development

Dezzio has partnered with the University of Newcastle to experiment with all the current Vision based Machine Learning models that will help with digitally enhancing images.

What we have learnt

Machine Learning techniques can work quite well for the untrained eye in the hands of a consumer, but for trained professionals there is a lot less tolerance for delivering an incomplete job, hence an inordiate amount of R&D is required to tackle the challenges in our industry.
We understand our industry data very well now, we have been doing vast amounts of pre-processing to prepare the data in a way that will lead to the best possible result, but more work is required to provide a service that will serve our marketplace well.
